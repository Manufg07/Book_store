List of Books

1.Macbeth by William Shakespeare

Enter the dark and captivating world of Shakespeare's 'Macbeth,' where ambition clashes with morality in a tragic tale of power and deceit. Follow the descent of the ambitious Scottish general Macbeth, driven by the prophecies of three mysterious witches and the relentless persuasion of his wife, as he navigates a treacherous path toward the throne. Explore themes of guilt, fate, and the consequences of unchecked ambition in this timeless masterpiece, available now on our online book store.

2.Romeo and Juliet by William Shakespeare

Discover Shakespeare's iconic tale of love and tragedy in 'Romeo and Juliet.' Set in Verona, this timeless story follows the fateful romance between two young lovers from feuding families. Witness the passion, drama, and heartbreak as Romeo and Juliet defy the odds in a world consumed by hatred. Explore themes of love, fate, and societal expectations in this enduring classic, available now on our online book store.

3.Oliver Twist by Charles Dickens:

Dive into the timeless classic 'Oliver Twist' by Charles Dickens. Follow the journey of young Oliver, an orphan navigating the harsh streets of Victorian London. From the workhouse to the underworld, Oliver's tale is one of resilience, friendship, and the fight against injustice. Discover Dickens' vivid characters and gripping storytelling in this unforgettable narrative of hope and redemption.

4.Harry Potter series by J.K. Rowling:

Step into the enchanting world of magic and adventure with the 'Harry Potter' series by J.K. Rowling. Join Harry Potter, a young wizard, as he discovers his true heritage and embarks on a thrilling journey at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. From battling dark forces to forging lifelong friendships, Harry's story captivates readers of all ages with its richly imagined universe and timeless themes of courage, friendship, and the power of love.

5.War and Peace by Leo Tolstoy:

Immerse yourself in Leo Tolstoy's epic masterpiece, 'War and Peace.' Set against the backdrop of Napoleonic wars, this sweeping saga follows the lives of Russian aristocrats, soldiers, and peasants as they navigate love, loss, and the tumult of history. With its intricate plot, profound philosophical insights, and richly drawn characters, 'War and Peace' remains one of the greatest novels ever written, offering readers a panoramic view of human existence and the enduring quest for meaning.
